About Us

Prince Ifetike Chekwube Foundation is committed to empowering communities, promoting climate action, and providing opportunities through education, innovation, and environmental sustainability programs.

Our Vision & Mission

🌍 Vision

To build a sustainable and inclusive society where communities are empowered, young people are equipped with knowledge and skills, and the environment is protected.

🎯 Mission

To drive community development through climate education, environmental sustainability, and youth empowerment programs that inspire action and long-term impact.

Our Objectives

Climate Education

Promote environmental awareness in schools through SDGs programs.

Waste & Recycling

Reduce pollution through plastic recovery and recycling initiatives.

Youth Empowerment

Equip young people with digital and vocational skills.

Community Development

Support underserved communities with sustainable programs.

Advocacy

Drive awareness and action on climate and sustainability issues.
